ABOUT MOBFIX

MobFix was created to accept the day to day importance of all hand held gadgets that keep us connected to world virtually. Our team realised with insatiable appetite of India towards smartphones was on the rise and progressive research enabled us to gain knowledge of that there are about 650 million mobile phone users in India, and just over 300 million of them have a smartphone. We would like to share the information of knowing there are more than 100 smartphone brands catering to a variety of budgets in India and that we are on par with United States of America and second to China. (Information source: CNN News)
The MobFix team realises the mammoth growth of Smartphones and its related services are just going to skyrocket in the next decade with about a billion people still not using smartphones and a potential +176 Million are going to grab the smart phones in next 5 years.
We put in lot of research and collected data in designing a unique innovative online technology we all proudly call MobFix . We ensure it benefits all the mobile phone and device users and especially the ever increasing numbers of smartphone users.
On the other hand we are creating an opportunity to the new generation of skilled technicians with an opportunity to earn, learn and deliver the best service through MobFix. We are creating opportunity of employment to thousands technicians aspiring to make mobile phone repair service industry strong and sustainable. We value the skillset on hand and want to ensure we create opportunities to all on the basic fundamental of collaboration rather than competition. We believe in grooming them, training them and ensuring our techs are learning and growing as the part of ever growing family.
MobFix realises the importance of Customers, their mobile phones/devices and most importantly the service it would need to maintain and repair these amazing smart devices time to time for our customers. Our team is here ready to serve at click of a button and looking forward to deliver Skill to service, Sustainability to Skill and Satisfaction in delivering both.
